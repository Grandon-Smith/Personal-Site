INTRO SECTION

I'd always had an interest in technology and building things. I had a construction orieneted mindset from the time I was a little kid. I was never great at math growing up, I wrote off any kind of coding for fear I wouldn't be capable of a career. After studying health sciences in undergrad, I applied to, and was accepted to several graduate programs for physical therapy. After a year of attending, I ultimately decided I was not following the right path for me. While I was in school though, I had discovered codecademy, which sparked my interest and initially got me hooked on coding. I juggled working and coding in my free time using resources like codecademy, the odin project, and freecodecamp. After moving home to Connecticut again in July 2020 (where I'm currently located), I decided now was my best chance to really see what coding was about, so I enrolled in Thinkful's remote coding bootcamp, and here I am now!
